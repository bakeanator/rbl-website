RBL Entertainment - or rebel - is a collective of filmmakers and business innovators dedicated to building impactful, globally resonant storytelling through true collaboration.

RBL Entertainment - or rebel - is a collective of filmmakers and business innovators dedicated to building impactful, globally resonant storytelling through true collaboration.

By integrating film finance expertise with a creator-first approach, RBL empowers storytellers to protect their vision while navigating the evolving demands of the global marketplace.

01 / Mission

To empower storytellers with the strategy, expertise, and access they need to bring bold, commercially viable projects to life - without sacrificing creative vision.

02 / Vision

A healthier, more equitable industry where independent voices thrive, great stories get made, and everyone is treated with the dignity they deserve.

03 / What we believe

Four core values that shape every decision we make.

#1

Creator First

Every decision begins with protecting and amplifying the storyteller's voice. We are advocates for the creative, always.

#2

Strategic Integrity

We don't separate art from business - we believe the best projects are the ones where both are handled with equal care and rigor.

#3

True Collaboration

We reject the gatekeeping, hierarchy, and isolation of old Hollywood. The best work happens when the right people are in the room together.

#4

Sustainable Filmmaking

Great stories should be made in ways that are financially sound, creatively fulfilling, and humanely executed - for everyone involved.
